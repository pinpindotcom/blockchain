Our Campaign

Welcome to Blockchain Database – Empowering Recovery, Ensuring Trust.

A Mission Born in 2022

In 2022, Blockchain Database launched a campaign dedicated to addressing one of the most pressing issues in the Bitcoin ecosystem: the growing number of lost and frozen wallets.
Bitcoin has a fixed supply of 21,000,000 coins, of which 19,917,700 have already been mined. However, approximately 4.7 million BTC are believed to be inaccessible, sitting dormant in wallets that have shown no activity since their creation. Many of these wallets are considered lost forever. This reduces network efficiency and adds volatility to the asset’s price due to a lower circulating supply.

Why We Started This Campaign

Our goal is simple: recover frozen and inactive Bitcoin wallets and help return lost funds to their rightful owners. By doing so, we aim to reduce unnecessary network congestion and maintain a more balanced BTC economy.
We do this without profit, without fees, and without taking any percentage of the recovered funds. Blockchain Database is a non-profit initiative built on transparency, trust, and community contribution.

How We Work

To make this possible, we collaborate with leading cryptocurrency exchanges to gather any available information related to potentially lost or inactive wallets. These partnerships allow us to assess wallet activity and begin the process of verifying ownership where recovery may be feasible.
It is important to understand that self-custodial wallets (wallets fully controlled by the user, without third-party involvement) cannot be recovered or traced, even with the assistance of exchanges. In such cases, the security model of blockchain technology itself limits our ability to help.

Getting in Touch

While we operate with open communication, we do not take unsolicited requests. If you have lost access to your Bitcoin wallet, you may reach out to us, but we are the ones who initiate any formal recovery process.
To verify the identity of any Blockchain Database representative, or for general inquiries, please contact our official support email: support@blockchaindatabase.email
You can also view all our high-ranking personnel directly on our website to confirm legitimacy before engaging in any communication.

Protecting the past. Reclaiming the future.
